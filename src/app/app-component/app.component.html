Sirona information

  • What is Sirona? Sirona helps users track their sleep, diet, and mood trends over time in order to provide a useful analysis that will help them improve their wellbeing. To achieve this, meanginful trends and correlations are extrapoloted from the aquired data and shown to the user, helping to identify negative affectors or to just simply track their progress.
  • How to use: Simple! Just add either a Mood, Sleep, or Diet log entry whenever you feel and Sirona will keep tabs on your progress. Don't feel like adding an entry today or filling out a whole log? Don't worry about it - Sirona will work what you give it. Although keep in mind that analysis will be more accurate the more data it has to work with! Make your first entry now!
